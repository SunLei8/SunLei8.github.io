        <h3><strong>Welcome Message</strong></h3><p>A workshop designed to bring the evolution of the finite-discrete element method (FEDM) from its beginnings to the latest advancements in this space, all while bridging the gap between industry and academia.</p><p>We will be hosting a series of presentations from leading global FDEM experts covering aspects related hydraulic fracturing in unconventional reservoirs, slope stability in mining, blast modeling, tunneling stability, CCUS, and nuclear storage.</p><p>All workshop presenters and attendees will also have the opportunity to submit a contribution to a featured journal issue on FDEM that will be published on JRMGE in 2023.</span></p>
